# Contributor Rights Assignment

Status:
- Mandatory for all contributions to this repository.

Effective date:

## Purpose

This document defines the contributor rights assignment requirement for ECU Forge.

The project is licensed under AGPL-3.0-or-later, and contributions are accepted only if the contributor signs the assignment terms below.

## Assignment Terms

By signing this assignment, the contributor agrees that:

1. The contributor assigns to the ECU Forge project owner all right, title, and interest in and to the submitted contribution, including associated copyrights, to the maximum extent permitted by applicable law.
2. The contributor has the legal authority to submit and assign these rights.
3. The contribution is an original work of the contributor or the contributor has sufficient rights to submit it.
4. The contributor understands that the project may use, modify, relicense, and distribute the contribution as part of the project.
5. The contributor represents that no confidential or third-party proprietary material is knowingly included without permission.
